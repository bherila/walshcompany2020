Welcome

The Walsh Company, LLC is a multi-service organization that specializes in general contracting, client representation and project management services. Founded in 2003 by Edward S. Walsh, The Walsh Company has developed into a successful enterprise of highly accomplished professionals headquartered in Morristown. As project specialists in  general contracting, project management and construction management, we understand the full range of challenges involved throughout the project process. Our broad scope of services include site evaluation, technical and operational lease consulting, design review, value engineering, cost estimating and pre-construction services, construction administration, furniture procurement services, and relocation coordination. Drawing on our extensive knowledge and expertise, we are able to execute all phases of our clients' projects from start to finish.
